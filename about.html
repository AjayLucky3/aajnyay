Our Story

About AajNyay

AajNyay was founded in New Delhi with a simple mission: to build "India's Legal GPS" and make legal awareness accessible to every citizen, regardless of language, education, or background.

Why "Legal GPS"?

The traditional legal model relies on complex documents, statutory citations, and manual Q&A loops. When faced with a dispute (like unpaid salary, rental deposits, or digital scams), citizens often do not know their rights or where to file complaints.

AajNyay transforms this by acting like a map system. By asking simple clarifying questions about your scenario, our engine routes you through local dispute policies, documents checklists, and contacts, presenting you with a personalized PDF escalation roadmap.

Trust & Compliance

Legal guidance demands extreme reliability. Every checklist, timeline, and authority map rendered by AajNyay has been compiled directly from verified government guidelines, RBI circulars, the Consumer Protection Act, 2019, the POSH Act, 2013, and municipal renting frameworks.

While AajNyay provides educational guidance and does not replace professional legal advocates, it gives you a firm foundation of facts, helping you avoid common mistakes and stand up for your statutory rights.

Development Phases

Our Project Roadmap

We are continuously upgrading our system to include next-generation AI and localized tools.

Phase 1 (Completed)

Interactive GPS Core

Developing offline decision trees, 10 primary guided case templates, document checklists, and local authorities directory.

Phase 2 (Q3 2026)

Linking citizens directly with verified local legal aid centers, panel attorneys, and consumer advocates for formal representation.
