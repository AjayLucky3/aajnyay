Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

At AajNyay, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information in connection with your use of our platform.

1. Data Processing and Storage

In our current deployment, all conversational logs in the AI Navigator and answers entered in the Legal GPS are processed locally in your browser session. We do not transmit, upload, or store your sensitive transaction figures, names, or dispute descriptions on our servers. Your data is stored in your temporary browser state and is destroyed when you refresh or close the page.

2. Cookies and Logs

We use local storage (localStorage) solely to persist your UI preferences, such as your selected dark/light theme state. We do not use tracking pixels, analytics, or behavioral cookie scripts.

3. External Links

Our platform references verified government portals, such as cybercrime.gov.in, consumerhelpline.gov.in, and the e-Daakhil filing board. Once you leave AajNyay, your actions are governed by the privacy statements of those respective official resources.

4. Compliance with DPDP Act, 2023

AajNyay adheres to the standards of the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023. Since we do not systematically collect or upload personal data, we do not perform profiling. If future updates incorporate LLM cloud loops or registry profiles, we will request your explicit, granular consent first.

5. Contact

For privacy queries, contact support@aajnyay.org.
